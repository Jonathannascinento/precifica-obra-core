# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of identity,
background, experience, or profession.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss technical decisions with evidence and empathy.
- Welcome questions and constructive review.
- Respect privacy, intellectual property, and professional responsibilities.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through the repository's security contact. The maintainer
may edit or remove content and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.
